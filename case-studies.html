Transforming PPC into a Lead Machine for a Residential HVAC Provider

The Challenge

A small residential HVAC business was struggling with self-managed Google Ads campaigns that delivered high-cost, low-quality leads. Without proper conversion tracking, the owner couldn't measure ROI, and generalized ad groups led to wasted spend on irrelevant traffic. The goal was to generate enough high-margin installation leads to support business growth without exceeding capacity limits.

The Strategy

The campaign started with a thorough account audit, installing conversion tracking for forms and calls. Keyword research focused on proven, high-intent terms, while adding negative keywords and splitting ads by territory and unit type (e.g., heat pumps) to achieve quality scores of 9-10/10. Custom landing pages were created for specific searches, featuring compelling offers, testimonials, and A/B testing for optimization. Ongoing monthly tweaks paused underperformers, added new keywords, and adjusted bids seasonally—such as boosting AC terms during heatwaves with timely promotions.

The Results

Within 24 months, the business scaled to 125 leads per month at under $15 per lead, yielding $18.38 in profit for every $1 spent (after ad budget and fees). This enabled hiring additional staff and purchasing more trucks to handle the demand, shifting focus from repairs to profitable installations.

Boosting Commercial HVAC Visibility Through Targeted SEO

The Challenge

A San Francisco-based commercial HVAC firm was losing market share to competitors dominating search results. Their website lacked visibility on Google and Bing, resulting in minimal organic traffic, few qualified leads, and inefficient marketing spend despite a need for more installations and revenue growth.

The Strategy

A specialized SEO approach was implemented to enhance search engine rankings and drive targeted organic traffic. This included optimizing site content for commercial queries, building backlinks, and improving on-page elements to secure top positions for key terms related to HVAC services.

The Results

The effort delivered a 177% increase in page-one keywords and a 116% rise in new web leads, empowering the company to capture more high-value commercial projects and strengthen its local dominance.

Year-Over-Year SEO Growth for a Competitive Regional HVAC Operation

The Challenge

In the saturated Central New Jersey market, an HVAC company faced stiff competition with a dated, non-mobile-friendly website that failed to rank on Google's first page. This led to stagnant traffic, lost opportunities to rivals, and inconsistent lead flow year-round.

The Strategy

A comprehensive digital overhaul combined SEO with paid media support, including a full website redesign for better user experience, mobile optimization, and security enhancements. Content was tailored to seasonal demands, with ongoing webmaster support to maintain rankings and integrate lead-generation tools.

The Results

Over three years, organic leads surged by 128%, while overall website traffic grew 162%, eliminating off-season slumps and setting new records for inquiries.
